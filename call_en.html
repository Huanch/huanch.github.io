My role

Requirement analysis, ux research, interface design


Project time：2018.12

We designed and built a new call center system so that the agents can work with multiple cases in one call.


The background

Our call center needed a call center system

In our call center, each agent served a specific business line. If a customer was involved with more than one business line, the customer had to be reached by more than one agent with multiple phone calls.

We needed a call center system, which could aggregate all tasks involved with one customer in one place, so that an agent can handel multiple cases in one call and a customer can recieve less calls from the company.

The start

Who are we designing for and what to design？

We started with analysing the stakeholder and define the use cases for MVP.

Stakeholder analysis


I discussed and mapped the stakeholders with product manager.




The users/ stakeholders and their influnce to the system are：

  • After-sell manager-manage the after sale business.
  • After-sell business operator- assign call tasks to call center team.
  • Call center team manager- assigne call tasks to agents.
  • Call center agent-excute the call tasks

The MVP covers the most important business cases from call center team manager and call center agent.

Call center agent

The concept

We defined the solution concept on whiteboard

With product managers we discuss requirments and tried different solutions on whiteboard.

The design challenge

Design for efficieny and adaptability

The design goal in the project is to make the system efficient to use and easy to be adapted. For reaching the goal, we need to solve following design challenges:

  • How might we help agent better manage the tasks- the system should adapt user’s working habit and mental modal
  • How might we make the agent working more smoonthly- the system can be enlarged for other business cases in future
  • How might we make a new agent adapt to job fast- the employee replacement is frequent in call center.

We defined our basic design principle while exploring possiblle solutions:

  • contextual- the system should adapt user’s working habit and mental modal
  • Scable- the system can be enlarged for other business cases in future
The reasearch

A field study to understand deeply how agents work

I took a field study in call center to understand the working context and the user behavior.

I analyzed the spreadsheet and observed how agents communicated with customers and updated information.



Outcome of the study
