Call center 1.0

Increase the operational efficiency

My role

Requirement analysis, ux research, interface design


Project time：2018.12

We designed and built a new call center system so that the agents can work with multiple cases in one call.


The background

We need a system to support the transformation of outbound customer interaction.

In our call center, each agent served a specific business line. If a customer was involved with more than one business line, the customer had to be reached by more than one agent with multiple phone calls.

We needed a call center system, which could aggregate all tasks involved with one customer in one place, so that an agent can handel multiple cases in one call and a customer can recieve less calls from the company.

The start

Who are we designing for and what to design？

We started with analysing the stakeholder and define the use cases for MVP.

Stakeholder analysis


I discussed and mapped the stakeholders with product manager.




The users/ stakeholders and their influnce to the system are：

  • Business line manager- He/She cares about how his/her business goes.They expect call center deliver customer information in time.
  • Business line operator- Assign customer list and customer inquiry to call center team.
  • Call center team manager- Assign customers to agents with the system.
  • Call center agent-Handle the customer inquiry through phone call with the system.

The business cases of MVP


The MVP covers the most important business cases from call center team manager and call center agent.

Call center team manager

  • Assign customers to agents.

Call center agent

  • Browser the customer list.
  • Solve the cases.
Solution exploration

We discussed ideas on whiteboard

The problem space

Design for efficieny and adaptability

After we have defined the basic structure of the system, we started to explore the UI solutions and following problems were what we need to solve in the project:

  • How might we help the agent to identify which customer to call efficiently?
  • How might we make the agent to solve the cases in time?
  • How might we make the agent interact and update feedback at the same efficently?
