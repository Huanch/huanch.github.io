Call managment system

Help after-sell team to increase the efficiency of customer service

Project time：2018.12

About the product

It is a system for after-sell service team to manage customers' data related to service/product such as insurance

We started with defining the stakeholders and business goals of the system

It is a system designed from scratch, so it is important to know the stakeholders at the beginning.



The users/ stakeholders and their influnce to the system are：

  • After sale manager- obtain performance data
  • Business operator- define business rules
  • Manager- assigne tasks to team members
  • Staff- solve the cases

The high-level goals of the product should reach are：

  • A customer is severed by only one staff all the time.
  • A staff can resolve multiple cases by one call

We explored ideas on whiteboard

With product managers we discuss requirments and tried different solutions on whiteboard.

Design principle defined

We defined our design principles according to our design goal, user bahavior and system complexity.

  • User adaption- the system should adapt user’s working habit and mental modal
  • Business impact- the system should help to collect important business data
  • Scablity- the system can be enlarged for other business cases in future

Early design

We created our early design with our undertanding assumption of how users work.

We discussed how the panel of the cases should be displayed.

Refine our design with users

I went to the call center to observe how users actually work，take records of their dialogues and analyzed their behavior pattern.


We redesigned the form with users.

Optimize the visual hierachy

Humans can process approximately 7 “chunks” of information at a time. I divided the supportive information into several segments. Each of them contains no more than 7 attributes of information.
