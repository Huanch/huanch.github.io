Usability testing for MOPSI

MOPSI is a location based information system developed by SIPU lab, Computer science department, University of Eastern Finland. I worked as an Usability researcher for MOPSI for 6 months.

This page tells usability tests I design and conduct for MOPSI

Research design

The research following the ISO 9204 principle

"The effectiveness, efficiency and satisfaction with which specified users achieve specified goals in particular environments. "

I defined several user tasks for testing.

Conducting the tests

6 users took the testing.

  • 3 international student and 3 locals
  • none of them has experience in using the system before

Tests were conduct at home of moderator(Me) or home of users.

Users were allowed to explore the system before testing.

No training was given to users.
