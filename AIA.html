Clinic management system for AIA

We collaborated with business analysts from AIA HongKong office to design a system for process medical insurance with clinics.

Project time：2017.11-2017.12

Project scope

In thie project, we aimed to delivered an intial prototype for clinic end.

We used story mapping for discovering the touch points between clinic staff and others.

Requirement analysis

Storyboard

We used storyboarded the to-be scanarios for defining the conceptional solution and details to be discussed with business analysits.

Interview

We conducted user interviews in order to

  • Understand how a doctor/nurse/operator/AIA works
  • Understand how a clinic works
  • Discover doctor and nurse’s motivation and needs in using AIA’s system

"The information given by the system must be easy to be interpreted" - Nurse

Solution

We delivered a deck of solution including interface design and process flow diagrams.

Example B: Invoice management
